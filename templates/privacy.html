Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Introduction

Downloza respects your privacy and is committed to protecting user information while providing a fast and free online video downloading service. This Privacy Policy explains how our website handles user data and how we protect your privacy.

Information We Collect

Downloza does not require user registration and does not collect personal information such as names, passwords, phone numbers, or addresses. Users can access and use the downloader anonymously.

Log Files

Like many websites, Downloza may collect basic log file information such as IP addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), referring pages, and timestamps. This information is used only for maintaining server performance, monitoring traffic, and improving the website experience.

Cookies

This website may use cookies to improve user experience, remember preferences, analyze traffic, and support advertising services. Users can disable cookies through their browser settings if they prefer.

Third-Party Advertising

Downloza may use third-party advertising services such as Google AdSense. These advertising partners may use cookies, JavaScript, or web beacons to display relevant advertisements based on user activity. These technologies automatically receive your IP address when ads are shown.

Downloza has no control over these cookies used by third-party advertisers. Users should review the privacy policies of these third-party services for more information about their practices.

Content and Downloads

Downloza does not host or permanently store downloaded videos. Temporary files created during the download process are automatically removed from the server after processing. The website only provides a tool that allows users to download publicly available content from supported platforms.

User Responsibility

Users are responsible for ensuring that their use of downloaded content complies with copyright laws and the terms of service of the original platform where the content was published.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

About Downloza

Downloza is a fast and free online video downloader that allows users to download publicly available videos from major social media platforms. The service works directly in your browser without requiring any software installation or account registration.

Supported Platforms

  • YouTube
  • Instagram
  • Facebook
  • Twitter (X)
  • Pinterest

Key Features

  • No login or account required
  • High-speed downloads
  • Multiple resolution options
  • Works on mobile, tablet and desktop
  • Simple and clean interface

Our Mission

Our goal is to provide a simple tool that helps users download publicly available videos for offline viewing. Downloza focuses on speed, privacy, and ease of use.
